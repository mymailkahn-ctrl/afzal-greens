Our Story

From a family plot to your plate

Afzal Greens began as a small family field on the edge of Rawalpindi, growing vegetables the way our elders always have — with patience and care for the soil.

As demand grew from restaurants and supermarkets for genuinely fresh greens, we replanted our fields around kale, rocket, basil and salad leaves — crops that reward speed from soil to table.

Today we harvest every morning for kitchens and homes across Islamabad & Rawalpindi, so what arrives at your door still tastes like it was picked an hour ago. Because, mostly, it was.
